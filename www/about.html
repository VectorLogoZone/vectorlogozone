Why SVG?

SVG stands for Scalable Vector Logo and are nothing more than simple plain text files that describe lines, curves, shapes, colors, and texts. What separates SVGâ€™s from the competition is that they are easily modified and can be manipulated via CSS or Javascript making them far more flexible and versatile than traditional PNG, GIF or JPGs.
